# Paid Media Auto-Purchase

### Overview

Paid Media Auto-Purchase automatically purchases and forwards paid media content from channels that require payment to view locked photos or videos. Perfect for aggregating premium content without manual intervention.

#### What It Does

* Detects paid/locked media in source messages
* Automatically purchases using Telegram Stars or subscription
* Forwards the unlocked content to your targets
* Tracks transactions automatically
* Works seamlessly in background

#### Key Benefits

✅ **Hands-free operation** - No manual purchases needed\
✅ **Complete automation** - Set and forget\
✅ **Transaction tracking** - See all purchases in history\
✅ **Works globally** - All your tasks automatically\
✅ **Respects limits** - Uses your Telegram spending settings

***

### How It Works

#### Processing Flow

1. **Message arrives** with paid/locked media
2. **Bot detects** content requires payment
3. **Auto-purchase** using your Telegram Stars balance
4. **Unlock content** and download media
5. **Forward** the unlocked media to targets
6. **Log transaction** in your credit history

**Processing time**: 2-5 seconds per paid media message

#### Payment Methods

Paid Media uses your **Telegram Stars** balance or channel subscriptions:

* **Telegram Stars**: Bot spends Stars from your account
* **Channel subscriptions**: If you're subscribed, content is included

**Important**: Ensure you have sufficient Telegram Stars balance before enabling.

### Getting Started

#### Prerequisites

1. ✅ Have Telegram Stars in your account (purchase via Telegram app)
2. ✅ Purchase Paid Media addon
3. ✅ Source channels use Telegram's paid media feature

#### Quick Setup

**Step 1: Purchase Addon**

1. Menu → My Addons
2. Find **Paid Media Auto-Purchase**
3. Tap **Unlock** and purchase with credits

**Step 2: Enable in Task**

1. Open your task settings
2. Find **Paid Media** toggle (usually in Advanced Settings)
3. Toggle **Paid Media** to ON
4. Save task

**That's it!**

The addon works automatically whenever paid media is encountered.

**You pay** for the paid media using your Telegram Stars balance.

#### Cost Example

```
Addon cost: 100 credits (for 30 days)
Per media cost: 10 Stars (set by channel)
100 paid photos forwarded = 1000 Stars spent

Total: 100 credits + 1000 Stars
```

#### Managing Spending

**Set limits in Telegram**:

1. Open Telegram Settings
2. Privacy & Security → Stars
3. Set spending limits

**Bot respects** your Telegram spending limits automatically.

### Troubleshooting

#### Issue: Paid Media Not Purchasing

**Check**:

* [ ] Paid Media addon is active (not expired)
* [ ] Paid Media toggle is ON in task
* [ ] You have Telegram Stars balance
* [ ] Source actually uses Telegram's paid media feature (not external payment)

**Fix**: Verify each item above, especially Stars balance.

***

#### Issue: Running Out of Stars

**Symptoms**: Bot stops purchasing after some messages

**Cause**: Telegram Stars balance depleted

**Fix**:

1. Open Telegram app
2. Go to Settings → Stars
3. Purchase more Stars
4. Bot resumes auto-purchasing

***

#### Issue: Spending Too Much

**Problem**: Addon purchasing more than you want

**Solutions**:

* **Set Telegram spending limits** (recommended)
* **Disable addon** in specific tasks
* **Monitor your Stars balance** regularly
* **Calculate expected costs** before enabling

**Prevention**: Test on one task with spending limit first, then expand.

***

#### Issue: Some Media Not Unlocking

**Possible causes**:

* Not paid media (bot can't unlock manually-restricted content)
* Channel uses external payment system (not Telegram Stars)
* Insufficient Stars at time of purchase
* Channel changed pricing after first detection

**Fix**: Verify channel uses Telegram's native paid media feature.

### Best Practices

#### Monitor Your Stars Balance

Check balance regularly, especially when forwarding from multiple premium sources.

#### Set Spending Limits

Use Telegram's built-in spending limits to prevent overspending:

* Settings → Stars → Spending Limit

#### Calculate Expected Costs

Before enabling:

1. Note channel's price per media (e.g., 10 Stars)
2. Estimate posts per day (e.g., 5 posts)
3. Calculate daily cost: 10 × 5 = 50 Stars/day
4. Monthly cost: 50 × 30 = 1500 Stars

#### Test on Limited Task First

Enable on one task with low volume to understand costs before scaling.

#### Track Transactions

Check your credit transaction history to monitor spending:

* Menu → Credit Transactions → Filter by "Paid Media"

#### Combine with Content Filters

Use Paid Media with media filters to only process specific content types (photos vs videos).

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Do I need a subscription to premium channels?

No, Paid Media works without subscriptions. It purchases individual posts using Stars.

**However**: If you DO have a subscription, bot uses that (no Stars spent).

#### Can I set a per-message spending limit?

Not directly in addon. Use Telegram's global spending limits instead.

#### What happens if I run out of Stars mid-forward?

Bot stops purchasing. Message forwards without media (or skips message entirely, depending on task settings).

#### Does it auto-refill my Stars?

No. You must manually purchase more Stars through Telegram app.

#### Can I get refunds on purchases?

No. Telegram Stars purchases are final. Bot spends as if you manually purchased.

#### Does it work with all paid media?

Only works with **Telegram's native paid media feature**. External payment systems (PayPal, credit cards) are not supported.

#### Can I review purchases before bot makes them?

No, that defeats automation purpose. Bot purchases immediately when paid media detected.

**Recommendation**: Use spending limits and test with small scale first.

#### What if channel changes pricing?

Bot pays new price automatically. Monitor your costs if channel changes pricing frequently.
