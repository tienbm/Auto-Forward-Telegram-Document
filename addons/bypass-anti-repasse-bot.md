# Bypass Anti-Repasse Bot

### Overview

Bypass Anti-Repasse Bot bypasses anti-forwarding protections that some channels use to prevent message redistribution. Perfect for forwarding from channels that block standard forwarding or use anti-forward bots like @antirepasse.

#### What Problem Does It Solve?

Some channels implement forward restrictions:

* Messages can't be forwarded normally
* Right-click forward is disabled
* Anti-forward bots (like @antirepasse) add restrictions
* Content protection mechanisms block redistribution

This addon bypasses those restrictions automatically.

#### Key Benefits

✅ **Transparent operation** - Works in background\
✅ **No configuration needed** - Simple toggle\
✅ **Works with popular anti-forward bots** - @antirepasse and similar\
✅ **Preserves content** - Forwards complete messages\
✅ **Fast processing** - No delay added

***

### How It Works

#### Technical Overview

When enabled, the bot:

1. Detects messages with forward restrictions
2. Uses alternative methods to copy content
3. Recreates message in target without "forwarded from" attribution
4. Bypasses anti-forward bot protections
5. Sends to targets seamlessly

**Processing time**: < 1 second (instant for users)

#### What Gets Bypassed

✅ **Standard forward blocks** - Messages marked as "unforwardable"\
✅ **Bot detection** - Anti-forward bots like @antirepasse\
✅ **Channel restrictions** - Forward-disabled channels\
✅ **Content protection** - Basic protection mechanisms

#### What Doesn't Get Bypassed

❌ **Copyright protection** - Legal restrictions (respect these!)\
❌ **Telegram server-level blocks** - Rare, system-wide restrictions\
❌ **Account-level bans** - If your bot account is banned from source

#### Issue: Still Can't Forward

**Check**:

* [ ] Bypass Anti-Repasse is enabled in task

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Is this legal?

**Technical answer**: This is a feature that bypasses technical restrictions.

**Legal answer**: Legality depends on:

* Your jurisdiction's copyright laws
* How you use the content
* Whether content is protected by copyright
* Your intent (personal vs. commercial)

**Recommendation**: Consult legal counsel if using commercially. Generally safe for personal use and aggregation with attribution.

#### Will my account get banned?

**Risk is low** if you:

* Don't abuse the feature
* Don't forward massive amounts
* Respect rate limits
* Don't violate Telegram ToS in other ways

**Risk increases** if you:

* Forward thousands of messages daily
* Get reported by content creators
* Use for spam or illegal purposes

#### Can source channels detect I'm bypassing?

**Generally no**. The bypass happens on your bot's end. Source channel sees normal bot activity.

**However**: If you forward their content publicly, they'll see their content in your channel (regardless of bypass detection).

#### Does it work on all anti-forward bots?

**Most common ones**: Yes, including @antirepasse and similar.

**All bots**: No guarantee. New anti-forward mechanisms may not be supported immediately.

**Updates**: Addon may be updated to support new anti-forward systems.

#### Can I disable for specific messages?

**No**. It's all-or-nothing per task. Enable/disable at task level only.

**Workaround**: Create two tasks - one with bypass, one without.

#### What happens when addon expires?

Messages from restricted sources will fail to forward. Your bot respects restrictions again.

#### Does it slow down forwarding?

**No**. Processing is instant. No noticeable delay.
