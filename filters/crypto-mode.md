**Crypto Mode: Automatic Filtering of Tokens and Contract Addresses**

Enhance your message filtering by automatically forwarding relevant content about tokens and contract addresses.

{% embed url="https://youtu.be/XSLx4O8tmTA" %}

&#x20;✅ **Crypto Mode Features:**

* **Contract Address Filtering:** Detects across multiple blockchains (e.g., BTC, ETH).
* **Fast Mode:** Increases filtering speed (disables editing/replies).
* **Contract Frequency Checker:** Analyzes contract appearance patterns.
* **Duplicate Detection:** Avoids repeated posts on the same contract.
* **Custom Header/Footer:** Adds context to forwarded messages.

⚙️ **Setup Guide**

1. **Navigate to Detail Task Forward**\
   Access the "Detail Task Forward" section.
2. **Select Crypto Mode**\
   Choose the desired crypto mode.
3. **Configure Parameters**\
   Set the required parameters according to your needs.
4. **Save**\
   Remember to save your configurations.
