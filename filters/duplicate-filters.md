**Anti-Duplicate Feature**

Eliminate duplicate messages with ease! Automatically prevent the forwarding of repeated messages to the same groups or channels, keeping your content spotless and spam-free.

{% embed url="https://youtu.be/lVT8Xy7yxm0" %}

### ✅ Key Features

* **Efficient Communication**: Perfect for sharing news, announcements, and promotions.
* **Reliable Delivery**: Automatically confirms prior message deliveries.
* **Simple Setup**: Easily activated within task settings.

### ⚙️ Setup Guide

1. **Navigate to "Detail Task Forward"**\
   Access this section to proceed.
2. **Select Duplicate Filters**\
   Choose this option as needed.
3. **Configure Parameters**\
   Set the required parameters based on your needs.
4. **Save**\
   Ensure you save your configurations.
