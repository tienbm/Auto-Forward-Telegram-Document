# 🔄 How to connect BOT?

{% hint style="success" %}
### **Download Mobile App or use Web**

✅ **iOS** → [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/autoforward-for-telegram/id6447486093)\
✅ **Android** → [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.autoforward.telegramforward)\
✅ **Web** → [web.autoforwardtelegram.com](https://web.autoforwardtelegram.com/)
{% endhint %}

#### 📲 Step 1: Launch the App or Web Version

After downloading the AutoForward Messages Telegram app or opening the web version, launch the application.

> ⚠️ Important: Make sure to log in using the same phone number that is registered with your Telegram account.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (231).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 🔐 Step 2: Enter Your Phone Number & Verify Code

1. Choose your country code, then enter your Telegram phone number.
2. Tap Continue to proceed.

> ✅ A login verification code will be sent to your Telegram app (not via SMS).

***

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (233).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

**✉️ Step 3: Enter the Verification Code**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (234).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once the code arrives:

* Open your Telegram app.
* You’ll receive a message with a login code from Telegram.
* Return to the AutoForward app/web and enter the code in the input field.

_Entering code in progress…_

_Enter the exact code to complete login._

> 🔒 Don’t worry – this process is 100% safe. Your phone number and login are securely handled via Telegram’s official API.

#### 🔒 Step 4: Enter Your Two-Step Verification Password (If Required)

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (236).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If your Telegram account has Two-Step Verification enabled, you will be prompted to enter your password after submitting the login code.

* Simply enter your password to complete the login process.

> 🔐 This is an additional layer of security set by you in Telegram.

> If you forgot your password, you can reset it via the Telegram app.

***

#### 🎉 Step 5: Connection Successful!

Congratulations! You’ve successfully connected your account to AutoForward Messages Telegram.

✅ You now have access to all features with a 7-day free trial to explore and experience the full power of the system.

Welcome aboard, and enjoy your automation journey!
