AI Mode is a powerful feature in AutoForward that brings artificial intelligence into your message forwarding workflows. It allows you to enhance, modify, or extract useful content from messages using advanced AI models — before those messages are automatically forwarded to your target channels, groups, or chats.<br>

This mode introduces automation supercharged with intelligence:

* Rewriting messages for clarity, tone, or style
* Extracting text from images with OCR
* Configuring creativity and output length to match your needs<br>

AI Mode gives users more control and flexibility, helping to maintain message quality, relevance, and impact — especially when dealing with large-scale forwarding, marketing, or content repurposing.

### 🧠 When to Use AI Mode

AI Mode is best suited for users who need more than just raw message forwarding — it’s designed to _refine, enhance, or extract_ valuable content automatically. Here are some ideal use cases:

#### ✅ Content Enhancement (Smart AI Rewrite)

* You want to rephrase or rewrite messages before forwarding them to make them more appealing or clearer.
* You’re forwarding content from external sources and want to remove branding or adjust the tone.
* You need to correct grammar or improve sentence structure for marketing, announcements, or auto-replies.

#### ✅ Extracting Text from Images (OCR Image AI)

* You’re forwarding screenshots, flyers, or visual content, and want to extract the text to reuse it or summarize it.
* You need searchable and editable text from images shared in Telegram groups or channels.
* You’re building an automated pipeline that reacts to text inside images (e.g., for alerts, data capture, summaries).

#### ✅ Maintaining Consistency

* You manage multiple channels and want to standardize message formats automatically.
* You want to filter or reword messages so they stay in line with your brand voice.
